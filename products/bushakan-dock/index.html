Bushakan Dock by DODOcase

Perfect for your nightstand, desk or front entry table, the Bushakan Dock is a safe place to keep your iPhone, glasses, spare change, jewelry or other miscellaneous personal items. Featuring outlets carved out for your charger, it reduces the mess of multiple cables. Each Bushakan stand is unique as it is made entirely from natural Maple or Walnut wood and an oil finish is applied to the stand to allow it to age naturally. Each one is handcrafted in San Francisco using FSC certified wood.
