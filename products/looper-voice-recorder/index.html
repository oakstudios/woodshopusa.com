Looper Voice Recorder by Brand New Noise

A voice recorder that is fun and easy to use for all ages. Hold down the red button to record, press the black button to play back, toggle the switch left for continuous play, and dial the knob to adjust the speed/pitch for fun. Record time is approximately 30 seconds. Made in the USA of hand-crafted and hand-painted sustainable American wood, non-toxic acrylic paint, and electronics.

Category: Gadgets Kids
Made by: Brand New Noise (brandnewnoise.com)
Location: Brooklyn, NY
Materials: birch poplar
