Christmas Blocks by Uncle Goose

Designed by House Industries, these 14 embossed blocks spell “merry christmas” or “happy holidays” with the two embossed sides, one side painted green, the other red. The other sides are adorned with stacking trees designed with deliberately minimalist brush strokes creating a seasonal message celebrating Whoevillesque holidays where love and beauty outweigh consumer clutter.

Made by: Uncle Goose House Industries (unclegoose.com)
